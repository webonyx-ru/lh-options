F.A.Q.

What are Binary options?

Binary option is a type of option in which the profit if you make the correct proposition of up or down price movement. The advantage is that you know for sure the exact size of you profit or loss when you enter the trade. Also you do not have to buy underlying asset itself.

What are available assets?

Whichever asset you choose: currencies, shares, indexes, metals, commodities and etc.

What is the strike price of an option?

It is the price where the security can be bought.

What is the expiration time?

It is the time, after which the options contract is no longer valid.

What are CALL and Put Option?

CALL option is purchased based on the proposition that underlying asset increases in price at the time of expiration. Alternatively Put options counts on the price decrease.

What is the initial deposit size?

We believe that it is the most adequate proposal for the beginners (as an opposite of some offers to start trading with USD 200-250). Of course the professionals may enlarge their account on any amount.

What is the lowest option price?

The lowest option price is USD 1 or 30 rubles depending on the currency of the account.

On what profit can I count?

When trading the classic binary options or turbo options your profit is from 60 up to 85-90% of the option rate in case of the correct forecast. The touch option may bring up to 170%. In the hottest trading hours you may count on profit up to 750%.

In case of the incorrect forecast you will lose only your option rate. You can secure your investments in order to lower losses. But in this case the percentage of you profit will be lower. The classical investment rule is applicable here: the lower risk gives lower profit.
